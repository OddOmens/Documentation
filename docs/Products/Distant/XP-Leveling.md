# XP & Leveling

Gamify your focus journey with experience points and levels.

## Experience Points (XP)

### Base XP

**Simple Formula:**
- 1 minute of focus = 1 XP
- 60-minute session = 60 XP
- Clear and straightforward

### XP Bonuses

**First Session of Day: +20%**
- Your first completed session each day
- Encourages daily practice
- Stacks with other bonuses

**Deep Focus: +25%**
- Sessions 60+ minutes
- Threshold customizable in settings
- Rewards longer focus periods

**Streak Bonus: +10%**
- Active streak of 3+ days
- Focus at least once per day
- Maintains streak

**Daily Goal Bonus: +15%**
- When you meet your daily goal
- Default: 60 minutes per day
- Customizable in settings

### Bonus Stacking

**All bonuses multiply together!**

**Example Calculation:**
- Base: 60 minutes = 60 XP
- First session: 60 × 1.20 = 72 XP
- Deep focus: 72 × 1.25 = 90 XP
- Streak: 90 × 1.10 = 99 XP
- Daily goal: 99 × 1.15 = 113.85 XP

**Maximum Multiplier:**
- All bonuses active: 1.8x base XP
- 60-minute session = 108 XP
- Significant reward for consistency!

### Overtime XP

**Bonus for Extra Time:**
- Continue past planned duration
- Earn XP for overtime minutes
- Same bonuses apply
- Rewards dedication

## Leveling System

### Focus Type Levels

**Individual Progression:**
- Each focus type has its own level
- XP earned goes to that specific type
- Track mastery in different areas
- 6 levels total

**Level Progression:**
1. **Level 1** - Beginner (0 XP)
2. **Level 2** - Novice (100 XP)
3. **Level 3** - Intermediate (300 XP)
4. **Level 4** - Advanced (600 XP)
5. **Level 5** - Expert (1000 XP)
6. **Level 6** - Master (1500 XP)

**XP Requirements:**
- Increases with each level
- More time needed for higher levels
- Sense of progression
- Long-term goals

### Level Naming Styles

Choose your preferred naming convention in settings.

**Traditional:**
- Beginner → Novice → Intermediate → Advanced → Expert → Master

**Competitive:**
- Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond → Obsidian

**Royalty:**
- Peasant → Commoner → Noble → Lord → King → Monarch

**Nature:**
- Seed → Sprout → Sapling → Tree → Redwood → Ancient Oak

**Zen:**
- Seeker → Student → Practitioner → Teacher → Master → Enlightened

**Gaming:**
- Noob → Casual → Regular → Veteran → Pro → Legend

### Overall Title

**Based on Total XP:**
- Combines XP from all focus types
- Shows overall mastery
- 10 ranks total

**Rank Progression:**

3. Intermediate (1500 XP)

5. Expert (5000 XP)

7. Sage (12000 XP)

## Strategies for Earning XP

### Maximize Bonuses

**Daily Routine:**
1. **First session bonus** - Start your day with focus
2. **Deep focus** - Aim for 60+ minute sessions
3. **Maintain streak** - Focus daily for 3+ days
4. **Hit daily goal** - Complete your target minutes

**Optimal Session:**
- 60+ minutes (deep focus)
- First of the day
- During active streak
- When daily goal will be met
- Earns 1.8x base XP!

### Consistent Practice

**Daily Focus:**
- At least one session per day
- Maintains streak bonus
- Builds habit
- Steady XP growth

**Longer Sessions:**
- 60+ minutes for deep focus bonus
- Don't burn out
- Quality over quantity
- Sustainable practice

### Diversify Focus Types

- Varied practice

**Strategy:**
