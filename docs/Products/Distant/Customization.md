## Tips for Customization

### Finding Your Style

1. **Start with defaults** - Use for a week
2. **Adjust gradually** - Change one thing at a time
3. **Test different options** - Try various combinations
4. **Settle on preferences** - What works for you

### Performance Considerations

- **Simpler animations** - Better battery life
- **Fewer notifications** - Less distraction
- **Minimal display** - Faster rendering
- **Balance aesthetics and function**

### Seasonal Changes

- **Summer:** Bright, vibrant gradients
- **Winter:** Cool, calm colors
- **Work mode:** Professional, minimal
- **Personal time:** Fun, energetic

### Accessibility

- **High contrast gradients** - Better visibility
- **Larger text** - Easier reading
- **Clear sounds** - Distinct notifications
- **Simple animations** - Less visual noise
