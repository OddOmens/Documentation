# Privacy & Data Management

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.

## Privacy-First Approach

### No Account Required
Start using Breatheful immediately without creating an account, providing an email, or sharing any personal information.

### Your Data Stays Yours
All your breathing session data, custom patterns, and preferences belong to you. We never access, sell, or share your personal information.

### No Tracking
We don't track your personal information, breathing habits, or app usage for advertising or analytics purposes.

### No Ads
Breatheful is completely ad-free. Your practice time is sacred and uninterrupted.

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
By default, all Breatheful data is stored locally on your iPhone or iPad:
- Breathing session history
- Custom breathing patterns
- Preferences and settings
- Streak and progress data

### iCloud Sync (Optional)

When you enable iCloud sync:
- Data is stored in **your personal iCloud account**
- Only you have access to this data
- Data syncs across all your devices signed in with the same Apple ID
- We never have access to your iCloud data

**What syncs:**
- Custom breathing patterns
- Session history
- Preferences and settings
- Streak data

**How to enable:**
1. Go to Settings in Breatheful
2. Toggle "iCloud Sync" on
3. Ensure iCloud is enabled on your device

## Data Security

### Encryption
- Data stored locally is protected by iOS device encryption
- iCloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We use Apple's secure frameworks for all data handling

### Device Security
Your data is as secure as your device:
- Use Face ID, Touch ID, or a strong passcode
- Keep your iOS updated
- Don't jailbreak your device

## What We Collect

### We DO NOT Collect:
- Personal identification information
- Email addresses or contact information
- Location data
- Breathing session details
- Usage analytics for advertising
- Any data that can identify you personally

### We MAY Collect (Anonymous):
- Crash reports to fix bugs (optional, can be disabled)
- Anonymous app performance metrics to improve stability
- This data cannot be linked back to you

## Your Rights

### Access Your Data
All your data is accessible within the app:
- View session history in the History tab
- See custom patterns in the Patterns section
- Check statistics in the Progress view

### Export Your Data
You can export your data at any time:
1. Go to Settings
2. Tap "Export Data"
3. Choose format (CSV or JSON)
4. Share or save to Files app

### Delete Your Data
You have complete control:
- Delete individual sessions from History
- Delete custom patterns anytime
- Clear all data in Settings > Clear All Data
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data

### Disable iCloud Sync
Turn off iCloud sync anytime:
1. Go to Settings
2. Toggle "iCloud Sync" off
3. Choose to keep or delete iCloud data

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services
Breatheful uses Apple's built-in frameworks:
- **iCloud** for optional data sync (your personal iCloud)
- **HealthKit** for optional health data integration (future feature)
- **Notifications** for breathing reminders

### No Third-Party Analytics
We don't use third-party analytics services like Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or similar tracking tools.

### No Third-Party Advertising
We don't use advertising networks or share data with advertisers.

## Children's Privacy

Breatheful is safe for all ages. We don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

If we make changes to how we handle data:
- We'll notify you through the app
- Continued use means acceptance of changes
- You can always delete your data and stop using the app

## Questions or Concerns

If you have questions about privacy or data handling:
- Contact us through the app Settings > Support
- Email us at [support email]
- We'll respond within 48 hours

## Transparency

We believe in complete transparency about data practices. If you have any questions about what data is collected, how it's stored, or how it's used, please don't hesitate to ask.

::: tip Your Privacy Matters
We built Breatheful with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought. Your breathing practice is personal, and we respect that.
:::

## Data Retention

- **Local data:** Retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **iCloud data:** Retained in your iCloud until you delete it
- **Crash reports:** Automatically deleted after 90 days
- **We don't retain:** Any personal or identifiable information

## Compliance

Breatheful complies with:
- Apple App Store privacy requirements
- iOS data protection guidelines
- General best practices for user privacy
