# Breatheful

**Breatheful** is a calm breathing and meditation app for iOS designed to help you reduce stress, improve focus, and enhance your overall well-being through guided breathing exercises and mindfulness practices.

[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/ng/app/breatheful-calm-breathing/id6451128943)

## What is Breatheful?

Breatheful provides a simple, elegant interface for practicing various breathing techniques backed by science. Whether you're looking to calm anxiety, improve sleep, boost energy, or simply take a mindful moment in your day, Breatheful offers customizable breathing exercises to meet your needs.

## Key Features

- **Multiple breathing patterns** including Box Breathing, 4-7-8 Breathing, and custom patterns
- **Visual and haptic guidance** to help you maintain rhythm
- **Customizable durations** to fit your schedule
- **Session tracking** and progress insights
- **Streak tracking** to build consistent habits
- **iCloud sync** across all your devices
- **No account required** - your privacy matters

## Wellness Benefits

- Reduce stress and anxiety
- Improve sleep quality
- Enhance focus and mental clarity
- Better emotional regulation
- Increase mindfulness and presence
