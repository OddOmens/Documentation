# Ideaful

**Ideaful** is a comprehensive iOS app for managing ideas, projects, tasks, and notes. Capture inspiration, organize your thoughts, track progress, and turn ideas into reality with powerful task management and customization features.

[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/ideaful)

## What is Ideaful?

Ideaful is your complete idea management system. From initial inspiration to final execution, Ideaful helps you capture, organize, and develop your ideas with tasks, notes, tags, and visual inspiration.

## Key Features

- **Idea Management** - Capture and organize unlimited ideas
- **Task System** - Break ideas into actionable tasks with priorities
- **Notes** - Unlimited rich text notes per idea
- **Visual Inspiration** - Add up to 10 images per idea
- **Tags** - Color-coded organization with 12 colors
- **Status Workflow** - 7 statuses from Planning to Completed
- **Smart Filtering** - Filter by tags, status, and priorities
- **Bulk Operations** - Tag or archive multiple ideas at once
- **iCloud Sync** - Automatic sync across all devices
- **Export** - Text, CSV, and Markdown formats

## Who is it for?

- Creative professionals capturing ideas
- Project managers organizing initiatives
- Entrepreneurs developing business concepts
- Writers planning stories and articles
- Developers tracking feature ideas
- Anyone with ideas to organize and execute
