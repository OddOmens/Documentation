# Expenses & Revenue

Track all your financial transactions.

## Expense Tracking

### Adding Expenses

**Manual Entry:**
1. Select wallet
2. Tap "Add Expense"
3. Enter details:
   - Merchant name
   - Item purchased
   - Quantity
   - Price (auto-calculates total)
   - Payment method
   - Category (50+ options)
   - Status (Paid/Unpaid)
   - Notes (optional)
4. Save

**Receipt Scanning:**
1. Select wallet
2. Tap "Scan Receipt"
3. Take photo or upload
4. Review extracted data
5. Edit if needed
6. Select items to save
7. Save

- And more...

### Payment Methods

**Supported types:**
- Cash
- Check
- Debit Card
- Credit Card
- PayPal
- Venmo
- Stripe

**Saved Cards:**
- Add payment cards once
- Quick selection for future expenses
- Track spending by card

### Expense Actions

**Swipe Actions:**
- **Delete** - Remove expense
- **Edit** - Modify details
- **Duplicate** - Copy expense
- **Mass Replicate** - Duplicate across months

**Mass Replication:**
1. Swipe on expense
2. Tap "Mass Replicate"
3. Select start month/year
4. Select end month/year
5. Confirm
6. Expense duplicated for each month

**Use cases:**
- Rent payments
- Loan payments
- Regular bills
- Predictable expenses

## Revenue Tracking

### Adding Revenue

1. Select wallet
2. Tap "Add Revenue"
3. Enter details:
   - Title
   - Source (25+ options)
   - Payment type
   - Amount or rate
   - Status
   - Notes
4. Save

### Payment Types

**Flat Rate:**
- Single payment amount
- One-time or recurring
- Simple tracking

**Hourly Rate:**
- Enter hourly rate
- Enter hours worked
- Auto-calculates total
- Perfect for freelance/contract work

### Revenue Sources

**25+ sources including:**
- Salary
- Freelance Income
- Consulting Fees
- Investment Returns
- Rental Income
- Business Revenue
- Commission
- Bonus
- Dividend
- Interest
- Royalty
- Grant
- Gift
- Refund
- And more...

### Revenue Status

Track payment status:
- **Unpaid** - Not yet received
- **Billable** - Ready to invoice
- **Invoiced** - Invoice sent
- **Pending** - Payment processing
- **Paid** - Payment received

### Revenue Actions

**Swipe Actions:**
- Delete
- Edit
- Duplicate
- Mass Replicate

**Tips:**
- Update status as payment progresses
- Use hourly rate for accurate tracking
- Mass replicate for regular income
- Track multiple income streams
