# Voice & Photos

Capture thoughts with voice recordings and photos.

## Voice Recordings

Record up to 5 minutes of audio with automatic transcription.

### Recording Audio

**Starting a Recording:**
1. Open a thought (or create new one)
2. Tap the **microphone icon**
3. Tap the **record button** (red circle)
4. Speak clearly into your device
5. Watch the waveform visualize your voice

**During Recording:**
- **Real-time waveform** shows audio levels
- **Timer** displays elapsed time
- **Progress bar** shows time remaining (5 min max)
- **Pause button** to temporarily stop
- **Stop button** to finish recording

**Finishing:**
1. Tap **stop** when done
2. Recording is automatically saved
3. Transcription begins immediately
4. Recording attaches to your thought

### Recording Features

**Waveform Visualization:**
- See your voice as you speak
- Visual feedback for audio levels
- Helps ensure good recording quality
- Real-time display

**Pause & Resume:**
- Pause recording temporarily
- Resume without starting over
- Useful for collecting thoughts
- Counts toward 5-minute limit

**Time Limit:**
- Maximum 5 minutes per recording
- Helps manage storage
- Encourages focused thoughts
- Timer shows remaining time

### Automatic Transcription

**How It Works:**
- Speech-to-text conversion
- Happens automatically after recording
- Uses iOS Speech Recognition
- Works offline once permission granted

**Transcription Features:**
- Appears below recording
- Copy to clipboard with one tap
- Saved with the recording
- Searchable text

**Accuracy:**
- Best in quiet environments
- Clear speech improves results
- Supports multiple languages
- May have minor errors

::: tip Better Transcriptions
For best transcription accuracy:
- Record in a quiet space
- Speak clearly and at normal pace
- Hold device close to mouth
- Avoid background noise
:::

### Managing Recordings

**Viewing Recordings:**
- Recordings appear in thought editor
- Shows recording name (date/time)
- Displays duration
- Shows transcription below

**Playing Recordings:**
1. Tap the recording card
2. Tap **play button**
3. Use **pause** to stop temporarily
4. Scrub through timeline
5. See playback progress

**Deleting Recordings:**
1. Tap recording card
2. Tap **delete button**
3. Confirm deletion
4. Recording removed permanently

**Recordings Gallery:**
- Access all recordings from menu
- See all recordings across thoughts
- Play any recording
- View associated thought
- Delete recordings

### Permissions Required

**Microphone Access:**
- Required to record audio
- Grant in iOS Settings
- Prompt appears on first use

**Speech Recognition:**
- Required for transcription
- Grant in iOS Settings
- Optional but recommended

**Granting Permissions:**
1. iOS prompts on first use
2. Or go to Settings → Privacy
3. Enable Microphone and Speech Recognition
4. Restart app if needed

## Photo Attachments

Add visual context to your thoughts with photos.

### Adding Photos

**From Photo Library:**
1. Open a thought
2. Tap the **photo icon**
3. Select "Choose from Library"
4. Pick one or more photos
5. Photos attach automatically

**Taking New Photo:**
1. Open a thought
2. Tap the **photo icon**
3. Select "Take Photo"
4. Capture photo with camera
5. Confirm to attach

**Multiple Photos:**
- Add multiple photos to one thought
- No limit on photo count
- Gallery layout in thought
- Swipe to view all photos

### Photo Features

**Automatic Compression:**
- Photos compressed for optimal storage
- Maintains good quality
- Saves device space
- Faster loading

**Gallery View:**
- Photos displayed in grid
- Tap to view full-screen
- Swipe between photos
- Pinch to zoom

**Full-Screen View:**
- Tap any photo to enlarge
- Swipe left/right for next/previous
- Pinch to zoom in/out
- Tap X to close

### Managing Photos

**Viewing Photos:**
- Photos appear in thought editor
- Grid layout for multiple photos
- Thumbnail previews
- Photo count indicator

**Deleting Photos:**
1. Open thought with photo
2. Tap photo to view full-screen
3. Tap **delete button**
4. Confirm deletion
5. Photo removed from thought

**Photo Storage:**
- Stored securely in Core Data
- Compressed automatically
- Backed up with device
- Not synced to cloud

### Display Options

**In List View:**
- Toggle "Show Photos" in settings
- Show/hide photos in thought cards
- Show/hide photo count indicators
- Customize your view

**In Thought Editor:**
- Photos always visible when editing
- Full gallery view
- Easy to add/remove
- Clear visual context

### Permissions Required

**Photo Library Access:**
- Required to attach photos
- Grant in iOS Settings
- Prompt appears on first use

**Camera Access:**
- Required to take new photos
- Grant in iOS Settings
- Optional if using library only

**Granting Permissions:**
1. iOS prompts on first use
2. Or go to Settings → Privacy → Photos
3. Select "All Photos" or "Selected Photos"
4. Restart app if needed

## Troubleshooting

### Recording Issues

**No Sound:**
- Check microphone permission
- Test microphone in other apps
- Check device volume
- Restart app

**Transcription Not Working:**
- Grant Speech Recognition permission
- Check internet connection (first time)
- Ensure clear audio
- Try re-recording

**Recording Cuts Off:**
- 5-minute limit reached
- Low storage space
- App interrupted
- Save and start new recording

### Photo Issues

**Can't Add Photos:**
- Check Photos permission
- Grant "All Photos" access
- Restart app
- Check storage space

**Photos Not Showing:**
- Toggle "Show Photos" in settings
- Check thought has photos attached
- Restart app
- Re-add photos

**Poor Quality:**
- Photos are compressed
- Original quality not preserved
- Use external photo app for editing
- Import edited version
