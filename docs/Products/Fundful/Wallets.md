# Wallets

Organize your finances into separate wallets.

## Wallet Types

### Personal Wallet
- Track personal finances
- Monitor personal spending
- Separate from business

### Business Wallet
- Manage business finances
- Choose business structure:
  - Sole Proprietorship
  - DBA (Doing Business As)
  - LLC (Limited Liability Company)
  - S Corporation
  - C Corporation
- Track business-specific metrics

### Category Wallet
- Organize by spending category
- Available categories:
  - Utilities, Groceries, Entertainment
  - Healthcare, Transportation, Housing
  - Education, Personal Care, Clothing
  - Dining, Travel, Gifts
  - And more...

## Creating Wallets

1. Tap "+" button
2. Enter wallet name
3. Select type
4. Choose structure (business) or category
5. Save

## Managing Wallets

### Viewing Wallet Details

**Summary Information:**
- Year-to-date income
- Lifetime income
- Net income (revenue - expenses)

**Transaction Lists:**
- All expenses
- All revenue
- All subscriptions

### Reordering Wallets

- Drag wallets to reorder
- Organize by priority
- Most-used wallets at top

### Editing Wallets

1. Swipe left on wallet
2. Tap "Edit"
3. Update details
4. Save changes

### Deleting Wallets

::: warning Data Loss
Deleting a wallet permanently removes all associated expenses, revenue, and subscriptions. This cannot be undone.
:::

1. Swipe left on wallet
2. Tap "Delete"
3. Confirm deletion

## Wallet Strategies

### Personal Finance

**Single Wallet:**
- One "Personal" wallet
- All personal transactions
- Simple tracking

**Multiple Wallets:**
- "Checking" wallet
- "Savings" wallet
- "Cash" wallet
- Track by account

### Business Finance

**Separate Business:**
- "Business" wallet
- All business transactions
- Clean separation from personal

**Multiple Businesses:**
- One wallet per business
- Track each separately
- Compare performance

### Category Budgeting

**Budget Categories:**
- "Groceries" wallet
- "Entertainment" wallet
- "Utilities" wallet
- Monitor spending per category
- Set limits

### Project Tracking

**Per-Project Wallets:**
- "Home Renovation" wallet
- "Vacation Fund" wallet
- "Wedding" wallet
- Track project-specific finances

## Tips

- Start with one wallet, add more as needed
- Use descriptive names
- Business wallets for tax tracking
- Category wallets for budgeting
- Review wallet totals regularly
