- Dark backgrounds create drama and focus

- Use complementary colors for contrast

## Background Effects

### Blur Effect
- Range: 0-50px
- Soften backgrounds, create depth
- 10-20px works well for most designs

### Color Adjustments

**Saturation (0-200%):**
- 0%: Grayscale
- 100%: Original
- 200%: Hyper-saturated

**Hue Rotation (0-360°):**
- Shift colors around color wheel
- 180° creates complementary colors

**Contrast (0-200%):**
- <100%: Softer, muted
- >100%: Bolder, dramatic

**Brightness (0-200%):**
- <100%: Darker
- >100%: Lighter

### Distortion Effects

**Twirl:**
- Spiral distortion from center
- Creates dynamic, swirling patterns

**Wave:**
- Horizontal wave distortion
- Adds movement and flow

**Ripple:**
- Circular ripple from center
- Water-like effect

**Zoom:**
- Scale background from center
- 100%: Original size

**Shake:**
- Random vibration effect
- Adds energy and motion

**Lens:**
- Barrel/pincushion distortion
- Fish-eye lens effect

### Effect Tips

- Combine multiple effects for unique looks
- Keep effects subtle for professional designs
- Bold effects work well for creative projects
- Reset individual effects with reset buttons

## Random Background

- Click dice icon for instant random selection
- Randomly chooses from all colors and gradients
- Great for inspiration and quick styling
