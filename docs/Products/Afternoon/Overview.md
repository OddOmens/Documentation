# Afternoon

**Afternoon** is a comprehensive iOS productivity app that combines habit tracking, task management, time blocking, and progress analytics into one unified experience. Built with SwiftUI and Core Data, it offers powerful features while maintaining a clean, intuitive interface.

[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/afternoon)

## What is Afternoon?

Afternoon helps you build better daily routines and stay organized by bringing together everything you need for personal productivity:

- Track daily habits with streak monitoring
- Manage tasks with automatic carry-over
- Plan your day with time blocks
- View detailed analytics and progress
- Integrate with Apple Health and Calendar
- Sync across all your devices

## Key Features

- **Habit Tracking** - Build streaks, track goals, and monitor progress
- **Task Management** - Automatic carry-over for incomplete tasks
- **Time Blocking** - Plan your day with visual timeline
- **6 Display Modes** - View your day the way that works for you
- **Analytics Dashboard** - Detailed insights and achievement system
- **Apple Health Integration** - Sync habit data automatically
- **Calendar Integration** - See all your commitments in one place
- **iCloud Sync** - Seamless backup across devices

## Who is it for?

- People building new habits or breaking old ones
- Anyone looking to be more organized and productive
- Users who want to visualize their daily schedule
- Those who love tracking progress and earning achievements
- People who want one app for habits, tasks, and time management
