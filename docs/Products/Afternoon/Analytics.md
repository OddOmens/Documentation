# Analytics & Progress

Track your progress, view detailed statistics, and earn achievements.

## Summary View

Quick overview of your productivity across different time periods.

### How to Access

Menu → Summary

### Time Periods

**Today:**
- Tasks completed today
- Habits completed today
- Progress tracking totals

**This Week:**
- Weekly task completion
- Weekly habit success rate
- Weekly progress totals

**This Month:**
- Monthly task completion
- Monthly habit success rate
- Monthly progress totals

**This Year:**
- Yearly task completion
- Yearly habit success rate
- Yearly progress totals

### Metrics Shown

**Task Completion:**
- "X of Y completed"
- Percentage complete
- Includes carried-over tasks

**Habit Success Rate:**
- Percentage of habits completed
- Excludes skipped habits
- Based on active habits only

**Progress Tracking:**
- Total for each measurement unit
- Aggregated across all goal-based habits
- Shown per time period

## Analytics Dashboard

Detailed statistics and insights about your habits.

### How to Access

Menu → Analytics

### Year Selector

- Choose any year from 2020-2050
- View habits active during that year
- Compare year-over-year progress
- Historical data preserved

### Summary Cards

**Total Completions:**
- All habit completions for selected year
- Includes all habits
- Shows consistency

**Average Success Rate:**
- Percentage of habits completed daily
- Requires 30+ days of membership
- Excludes skipped days
- Overall consistency metric

**Hottest Streak:**
- Your current active streak
- Any habit with ongoing streak
- Motivation to keep going

**Longest Streak:**
- All-time best streak
- Any habit, any time
- Personal record

**Total Missed:**
- Days habits were incomplete
- Doesn't include skipped
- Shows areas for improvement

**Total Skipped:**
- Days habits were intentionally skipped
- Rest days and planned skips
- Doesn't break streaks

### Per-Habit Analytics

Scroll down to see individual habit statistics:

**For Each Habit:**
- Completion rate (percentage)
- Current streak
- Longest streak
- Total completions
- Days active
- Visual progress charts

**Charts Include:**
- Daily completion patterns
- Weekly trends
- Monthly summaries
- Color-coded progress

::: tip 30-Day Requirement
Average success rate requires 30+ days of membership to display accurately. This ensures meaningful statistics.
:::

## Achievement System

Earn achievements for reaching milestones.

### Achievement Categories

#### 1. Habit Count

Unlock achievements for creating habits:
- 1, 3, 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30 habits
- Up to 100 habits
- Encourages habit diversity

#### 2. Success Rate

Unlock achievements for consistency:
- 10%, 20%, 30%... up to 100%
- Requires 30+ days of membership
- Measures overall consistency

#### 3. Streaks

Unlock achievements for consecutive days:
- 1, 3, 5, 7, 10, 14, 21, 30 days
- 50, 75, 100, 150, 200, 250, 300 days
- Up to 365 days (full year!)
- Celebrates dedication

#### 4. Progress Tracking

Unlock achievements for each measurement unit:
- First milestone: 1 unit
- Regular milestones: 5, 10, 15, 20... up to 1000
- Large milestones: 2k, 3k... up to 10k
- Tracks cumulative progress

### Viewing Achievements

**In Profile:**
- Shows 4 most recent achievements
- Tap "View All" for complete list

**In Achievement List:**
- Earned achievements shown in color
- Locked achievements shown in gray
- Each shows title, description, and icon
- Scroll to see all categories

### Achievement Notifications

**When Earned:**
- In-app notification appears
- Only for newly earned achievements
- Celebrates your progress

**Settings:**
- Can be disabled in Customize settings
- Menu → Customize → In-App Notifications
- Toggle "Achievement notifications"

## Understanding Your Data

### Success Rate

**What it means:**
- Percentage of habits completed each day
- Higher = more consistent
- 70%+ is excellent
- 50-70% is good
- Below 50% may indicate too many habits

**How to improve:**
- Reduce number of habits
- Make habits more specific
- Set realistic goals
- Use reminders
- Review and adjust

### Streaks

**What they mean:**
- Consecutive days of completion
- Shows consistency
- Builds momentum
- Creates habits

**How to build:**
- Start small (aim for 7 days)
- Use reminders
- Track daily
- Don't break the chain
- Use skip for rest days

### Completion Patterns

**Look for:**
- Days you're most consistent
- Times you complete most habits
- Habits you complete reliably
- Habits you often miss

**Use insights to:**
- Schedule habits at best times
- Identify problem habits
- Adjust goals
- Optimize routine

## Tips for Better Analytics

### Accurate Tracking

1. **Track honestly** - Don't mark incomplete habits as done
2. **Use skip appropriately** - For planned rest days only
3. **Complete daily** - Don't batch-complete past days
4. **Review regularly** - Check analytics weekly

### Setting Goals

1. **Use data** - Base goals on past performance
2. **Start small** - Increase gradually
3. **Be realistic** - Don't aim for 100% immediately
4. **Adjust as needed** - Lower if consistently missing

### Staying Motivated

1. **Check achievements** - See what you've unlocked
2. **View streaks** - Watch them grow
3. **Compare periods** - See improvement over time
4. **Celebrate milestones** - Acknowledge progress

### Long-Term Tracking

1. **Don't delete habits** - Archive instead
2. **Keep historical data** - Valuable for insights
3. **Review yearly** - See long-term trends
4. **Export data** - Take screenshots of analytics
