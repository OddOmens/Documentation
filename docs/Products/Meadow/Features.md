# Core Features

Complete guide to all Meadow features.

## 1. Conversations (Chats)

Track conversations with support people.

### What You Can Track

- **Title** - Name your conversation
- **Support Person** - Who you talked with
- **Date & Time** - When it happened
- **Pre-Mood** - How you felt before
- **Post-Mood** - How you felt after
- **Purpose** - What you discussed
- **Advice** - Key takeaways
- **Notes** - Additional reflections

### Views

- **Upcoming** - Next scheduled conversations
- **Past** - All previous conversations
- **See All** - Full conversation history

## 2. Goals (Treatment Plans)

Set and track personal growth goals.

### What You Can Track

- **Category** - Area this addresses (Anxiety, Career, etc.)
- **Goal** - What you want to achieve
- **Description** - Detailed plan and steps

### Tips

- Break large goals into specific steps
- Reference advice from conversations
- Update descriptions as you progress
- Review goals regularly

## 3. Thoughts

Capture reflections and insights.

### What You Can Track

- **Content** - Your thought or reflection
- **Category** - Type (Gratitude, Anxiety, Reflection, etc.)

### Use Cases

- Gratitude journal
- Worry log
- General reflection
- Daily insights

## 4. Support Network

Manage your support people.

### Supported Types

**Healthcare Providers:**
- Therapist, Psychologist, Psychiatrist
- Counselor, Social Worker, Nurse
- Nutritionist, Occupational Therapist, Physical Therapist

**Spiritual Leaders:**
- Priest, Pastor, Chaplain
- Rabbi, Imam, Spiritual Advisor

**Other Support:**
- Life Coach, Mentor, Professor
- Friend, Family, Support Group

### What You Can Track

- Name (first and last)
- Title (Dr., Rev., Prof., etc.)
- Type (their role)
- Profile Photo
- Contact Info (email, phone, website)
- Status (active or inactive)
- Notes

## 5. Profile & Settings

### Sync Status

- **Synced with iCloud** - Backing up and syncing
- **Local Storage Only** - Device only

### Customization

**Navigation Labels:**
- Toggle tab names on/off

**Custom App Icons:**
- 10 beautiful designs
- Default, Morning, Midnight, Winter, Charcoal
- Norse, Seaweed, Mint, Cherry Blossom, Blueberry

### Data Management

**Selective Deletion:**
- All Conversations
- All Goals
- All Thoughts
- All Support Network
- All Data

::: warning Permanent Deletion
Deletions remove data from both device AND iCloud. Cannot be undone.
:::

## Privacy & Data Storage

### Local-First Design

- All data stored locally on device
- Works 100% offline
- No internet required

### Optional iCloud Sync

- Automatic backup
- Sync across devices
- Encrypted and private
- You control it

## Tips for Getting the Most

### Building a Routine

1. **After Conversations** - Log immediately
2. **Daily Reflection** - Capture thoughts each evening
3. **Weekly Review** - Review goals and update
4. **Monthly Check-in** - Look back at patterns

### Effective Goal Setting

- Be specific
- Include action steps
- Reference advice
- Update regularly

### Meaningful Thought Tracking

- Write naturally
- Use categories consistently
- Capture both positive and challenging
- Reference specific events

### Managing Support Network

- Keep contact info updated
- Use notes for important details
- Mark inactive instead of deleting
- Add photos for personalization
