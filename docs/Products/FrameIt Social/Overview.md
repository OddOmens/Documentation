# FrameIt Social

**FrameIt Social** is a powerful web-based mockup creator designed to help you create stunning, professional mockups for social media, presentations, and marketing materials. Transform your screenshots and images into beautiful, shareable graphics in minutes.

[Visit FrameIt Social](https://frameit.social)

## What is FrameIt Social?

FrameIt Social is a browser-based tool that lets you create professional mockups without design experience. Upload your images, choose from 8 layouts, customize backgrounds, add text overlays, and export in multiple formats optimized for different platforms.

## Key Features

- **8 Professional Layouts** - Single image to 2×2 grids
- **120+ Colors & 100+ Gradients** - Extensive background options
- **Texture Overlays** - Paper, canvas, grain, and more
- **Text Layers** - 20+ Google Fonts with full styling
- **Image Watermarks** - Brand your mockups
- **Smart Export** - Optimized for Instagram, LinkedIn, and more
- **Batch Processing** - Export all canvases at once
- **Real-time Preview** - See changes instantly
- **No Account Required** - Start creating immediately

## Who is it for?

- Social media managers creating post graphics
- App developers showcasing screenshots
- Marketers creating promotional materials
- Content creators making thumbnails
- Designers prototyping layouts
- Anyone needing quick, professional mockups

## How It Works

1. **Upload images** - Drag and drop or click to upload
2. **Choose layout** - Select from 8 professional templates
3. **Customize** - Backgrounds, text, effects, and more
4. **Export** - Download in PNG, JPEG, or WebP
