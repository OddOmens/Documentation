# Meadow

**Meadow** is your personal mental health companion for iOS. Track conversations with support people, set goals, capture thoughts, and manage your support network - all in a private, local-first app.

[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/meadow)

## What is Meadow?

Meadow helps you organize and reflect on your mental health journey by providing tools to track conversations with therapists, coaches, mentors, friends, or family, while maintaining complete privacy.

- People in therapy or counseling
- Those working with life coaches or mentors

- People tracking personal growth

## Privacy First

**Local-First Design:**
- All data stored on your device
- Works 100% offline
- No account required
- No tracking or analytics
- Optional iCloud sync (encrypted and private)
