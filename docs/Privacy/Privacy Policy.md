# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

## Our Commitment to Privacy

At Odd Omens, we believe your data is yours. We design our apps with privacy as a core principle, not an afterthought. This policy explains how we handle your information across all our products.

## Information We Collect

### What We DON'T Collect

- **No personal identification information** - We don't collect names, emails, or contact details
- **No location tracking** - We don't track where you are
- **No usage analytics for advertising** - We don't track your behavior to sell ads
- **No third-party tracking** - We don't use analytics services that track you across apps
- **No data selling** - We will never sell your data to anyone

### What We DO Collect

**Crash Reports (Optional):**
- Anonymous crash logs to fix bugs
- Device type and iOS version
- No personally identifiable information
- Can be disabled in iOS Settings

**App Performance Metrics (Anonymous):**
- Basic app performance data
- Helps us improve stability
- Cannot be linked back to you
- Aggregated and anonymized

## How We Store Your Data

### Local Storage

**All our apps store data locally on your device:**
- Breatheful: Breathing sessions and preferences
- Afternoon: Habits, tasks, and analytics
- Depthful: Thoughts, recordings, and photos
- Distant: Focus sessions and achievements
- Fundful: Financial transactions and documents
- Ideaful: Ideas, tasks, and notes

**Your device security protects your data:**
- iOS encryption
- Face ID / Touch ID protection
- Secure by default

### iCloud Sync (Optional)

**When you enable iCloud sync:**
- Data stored in YOUR personal iCloud account
- We never have access to your iCloud data
- Encrypted in transit and at rest
- You control what syncs
- Can be disabled anytime

**What syncs:**
- App data and preferences
- User-created content
- Settings and customizations

**What doesn't sync:**
- Crash reports
- Performance metrics
- Device-specific settings

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services Only

We use Apple's built-in frameworks:
- **iCloud** - Optional data sync (your personal iCloud)
- **HealthKit** - Optional health data integration (Breatheful, Afternoon)
- **Screen Time API** - App blocking (Distant)
- **Speech Recognition** - Voice transcription (Depthful)
- **Notifications** - Reminders and alerts

**No third-party services:**
- No Google Analytics
- No Facebook SDK
- No advertising networks
- No tracking pixels
- No external databases

## Your Rights

### Access Your Data

All your data is accessible within each app:
- View all content you've created
- Export data in multiple formats
- No hidden data collection

### Export Your Data

**Available export formats:**
- CSV (spreadsheets)
- Text files
- Markdown
- JSON (where applicable)

**Export from:**
- Depthful: Thoughts and tags
- Fundful: Financial transactions
- Ideaful: Ideas and tasks
- Other apps: Check in-app settings

### Delete Your Data

**Complete control:**
- Delete individual items anytime
- Clear all data in app settings
- Uninstall app to remove all local data
- Delete iCloud data from iOS Settings

**What happens when you delete:**
- Local data removed immediately
- iCloud data removed from your account
- No data retained by us
- Cannot be recovered

## Children's Privacy

Our apps are safe for all ages:
- No data collection from children
- No age verification required
- No targeted content
- COPPA compliant

## Changes to Privacy Policy

**If we update this policy:**
- Notification in app updates
- Posted on this page
- Continued use means acceptance
- You can always delete data and stop using
