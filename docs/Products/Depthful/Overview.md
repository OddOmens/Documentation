# Depthful

**Depthful** is a thoughtful journaling app designed to help you capture, organize, and reflect on your thoughts, ideas, and experiences. With support for text, voice recordings, photos, and rich markdown formatting, Depthful provides a flexible and powerful platform for personal reflection.

[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/depthful)

## What is Depthful?

Depthful is your personal thinking space - a place to capture fleeting thoughts, record voice memos, attach photos, and organize everything with tags. Whether you're journaling daily, capturing ideas, or tracking dreams, Depthful adapts to your needs.

## Key Features

- **Rich Text Formatting** - Markdown support with formatting toolbar
- **Voice Recordings** - Up to 5 minutes with automatic transcription
- **Photo Attachments** - Add multiple photos to any thought
- **Flexible Tagging** - 18 default tags plus unlimited custom tags
- **Multi-Tag Support** - Apply multiple tags to organize thoughts
- **Analytics Dashboard** - Track writing patterns and streaks
- **Import/Export** - Text, Markdown, and CSV formats
- **Complete Privacy** - All data stored locally on your device

## Who is it for?

- Daily journalers building a writing habit
- Idea collectors capturing inspiration
- Dream trackers recording nightly visions
- Gratitude practitioners reflecting on positives
- Anyone who wants to organize their thoughts
