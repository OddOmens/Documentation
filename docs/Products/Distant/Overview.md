# Distant

**Distant** is a premium focus timer app designed to help you stay present and minimize phone distractions. Using Apple's Screen Time API, Distant blocks selected apps during focus sessions, helping you maintain deep concentration on what matters most.

[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/distant)

## What is Distant?

Distant combines a flexible timer with app blocking to create distraction-free focus sessions. Whether you're studying, working, meditating, or reading, Distant helps you stay in the zone by blocking the apps that pull you away.

## Key Features

- **Flexible Timer** - 1 to 240 minutes, or infinite focus mode
- **App Blocking** - Block distracting apps using Screen Time API
- **30+ Focus Types** - Reading, Working, Meditation, and more
- **Custom Focus Types** - Create your own with 93 icons and 13 colors
- **Gamification** - Earn XP, level up, unlock achievements
- **Overtime Mode** - Continue past your planned time for bonus XP
- **Infinite Focus** - Unlimited sessions with milestone notifications
- **Analytics Dashboard** - Track progress, streaks, and patterns
- **Deep Customization** - 50+ gradients, sounds, and visual options

## Who is it for?

- Students who need to focus on studying
- Professionals working on deep work tasks
- Anyone trying to reduce phone addiction
- People building better focus habits
- Meditators and mindfulness practitioners

## How It Works

1. **Set your duration** - Use the dial or presets
2. **Choose focus type** - What you'll be working on
3. **Block apps** - Select distracting apps to block
4. **Start focusing** - Apps are blocked, timer counts down
5. **Earn rewards** - Get XP, level up, unlock achievements
