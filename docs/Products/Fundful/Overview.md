# Fundful

**Fundful** is a comprehensive personal and business finance management app for iOS that helps you track income, expenses, subscriptions, assets, and financial goals across multiple wallets.

[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/fundful)

## What is Fundful?

Fundful is your complete financial tracking solution. Whether you're managing personal finances, running a business, or both, Fundful provides the tools you need to track every dollar, monitor subscriptions, calculate net worth, and achieve your financial goals.

## Key Features

- **Multiple Wallets** - Separate personal, business, and category finances
- **Expense Tracking** - 50+ categories with receipt scanning
- **Revenue Management** - Track income with flat or hourly rates
- **Subscription Tracking** - Auto-generate expenses on renewal dates
- **Asset Manager** - Calculate net worth with 10+ asset types
- **Financial Goals** - Set targets with progress tracking
- **Receipt Scanner** - OCR technology extracts transaction data
- **Document Locker** - Secure storage for financial documents
- **Financial Tools** - Calculators for loans, breakdowns, and currency
- **Reporting** - Export CSV reports for any time period

## Who is it for?

- Individuals tracking personal finances
- Freelancers managing income and expenses
- Small business owners tracking business finances
- Anyone wanting to monitor subscriptions
- People building emergency funds or savings goals
- Users tracking net worth and assets
