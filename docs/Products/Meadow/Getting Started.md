# Getting Started

Get up and running with Meadow in 5 minutes.

## First Launch

When you first open Meadow, you'll see the Chats tab with an empty state. The app is ready to use immediately - no account setup or configuration needed!

## 5-Minute Setup

### Step 1: Add Your First Support Person (30 seconds)

1. Tap the **Supporters** tab (4th icon)
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Select their type (Therapist, Friend, Coach, etc.)
4. Enter their first and last name
5. Tap checkmark to save

### Step 2: Log Your First Conversation (2 minutes)

1. Tap the **Chats** tab (1st icon)
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Add a title (e.g., "Weekly Check-in")
4. Select the support person
5. Fill in date and details
6. Tap checkmark to save

### Step 3: Set Your First Goal (1 minute)

1. Tap the **Goals** tab (2nd icon)
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Enter category (e.g., "Personal Growth")
4. Write your goal
5. Add description with action steps
6. Tap save

### Step 4: Capture Your First Thought (30 seconds)

1. Tap the **Thoughts** tab (3rd icon)
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Write what's on your mind
4. Add a category (optional)
5. Tap save

### Step 5: Check Your Profile (30 seconds)

1. Tap the **Myself** tab (5th icon)
2. See your sync status (iCloud or local-only)
3. Explore settings to customize

## Daily Usage

### After a Conversation
1. Open Meadow
2. Tap **Chats** → **+**
3. Log while it's fresh
4. Note pre/post mood

### Evening Reflection
1. Open Meadow
2. Tap **Thoughts** → **+**
3. Write about your day
4. Categorize the thought

### Weekly Review
1. Open **Goals** tab
2. Review your goals
3. Update progress
4. Add new goals

## Tips for Success

- **Be Consistent** - Log conversations right after they happen
- **Be Honest** - This is your private space
- **Be Specific** - Use descriptive titles and detailed notes
- **Be Patient** - Patterns emerge over time
