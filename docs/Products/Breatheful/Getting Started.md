# Getting Started with Breatheful

Get up and running with Breatheful in just a few minutes.

## Installation

1. **Download Breatheful** from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/ng/app/breatheful-calm-breathing/id6451128943)
2. **Open the app** - no account or sign-up required
3. **Grant permissions** (optional but recommended):
   - Notifications for breathing reminders
   - iCloud sync to backup your data

## Your First Session

1. **Choose a breathing exercise** from the home screen
2. **Adjust the duration** if needed (start with 2-3 minutes)
3. **Tap Start** and follow the visual guide
4. **Breathe in sync** with the animations
5. **Complete your session** and see your progress

## Recommended Settings

For the best experience:

- **Enable iCloud syncing** to keep your progress and custom patterns backed up
- **Allow notifications** to receive breathing reminders throughout the day
- **Use headphones** for immersive audio guidance
- **Find a quiet space** where you won't be interrupted

## Tips for Beginners

::: tip Start Small
Begin with just 2-3 minutes per day. Consistency matters more than duration. As breathing becomes a habit, gradually increase your session length.
:::

- **Practice at the same time each day** to build a routine
- **Start with guided patterns** before creating custom ones
- **Don't force it** - breathing should feel natural and comfortable
- **Be patient** - benefits increase with regular practice
